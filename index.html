Experience the Joy of Traveling on a Ferry

These comfortable and convenient vessels depart San Francisco from behind the historic San Francisco Ferry Building (foot of Market Street) and cruise to sunny Sausalito, Tiburon, and Larkspur in Marin County. Once underway, they offer dramatic views of the Golden Gate Bridge, Alcatraz, Angel Island, and the San Francisco skyline.
